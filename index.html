About me

I will design & build the best websites

I'm a UI/UX designer and creative developer who enjoys the whole journey — sketching a layout, picking type and color that actually fit the brand, then coding it into a real, responsive site. I like clean structure, small hover details, and pages that feel handcrafted instead of templated.

Most of my recent work has come out of university group projects, where I've handled both the design system and the front-end build — from product pages to documentation.
